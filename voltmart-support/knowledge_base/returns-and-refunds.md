# VoltMart Returns & Refunds Policy

## Return window
You have **30 days from the delivery date** to return most items for a refund. Opened items are accepted within this window as long as they meet the condition requirements below.

## Condition requirements
To be eligible for a refund, items must be:
- In their original packaging, with all accessories, cables, and manuals included.
- Free of physical damage that was not present on arrival.

Headphones and earbuds may be returned if used, but for hygiene reasons the ear tips must be the unused spares where provided.

## Non-returnable items
- Gift cards.
- Software, digital downloads, and redeemed license keys.
- Items marked "Final Sale" on the product page.

## How to start a return
1. Go to "My Orders" and select the item you want to return.
2. Choose a reason and print the prepaid return label.
3. Drop the package at any partner carrier location.

## Refund process
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are issued to the **original payment method** within **5–7 business days**. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to a VoltMart error (wrong or defective item). Refund approvals and any exceptions outside this policy are handled by a VoltMart support specialist.
