# VoltMart Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Where we ship
VoltMart ships to all 50 US states and to Canada. We do not currently ship to other countries.

## Delivery timeframes
- **Standard shipping:** 3–5 business days.
- **Express shipping:** 1–2 business days.
- **Same-day delivery:** available in select metro areas for orders placed before 12:00 PM local time.

Orders are processed within 1 business day. You will receive a tracking link by email once your order ships.

## Shipping costs
- **Standard shipping** is **free** on orders over **$50**. For orders under $50, standard shipping is a flat **$4.99**.
- **Express shipping** is a flat **$12.99**.
- **Same-day delivery** is **$19.99**.

## Regions and surcharges
Deliveries to Alaska, Hawaii, and remote postal codes may take 2–3 additional business days and may incur a $9.99 remote-area surcharge. Canadian orders may be subject to import duties collected at delivery.

## Tracking
You can track an order from the "My Orders" page in your account, or by giving our support assistant your order number and the email on your account.
