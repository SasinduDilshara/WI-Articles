# VoltMart Payment Methods & Billing FAQ

## Accepted payment methods
- Major credit and debit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover.
- PayPal.
- VoltMart gift cards and store credit.
- "Pay in 4" interest-free installments on eligible orders over $100.

## When you are charged
For in-stock items, your card is charged when the order ships. For pre-orders, you are charged when the item is ready to ship, not at the time of order.

## Billing FAQ
- **Why do I see a temporary authorization hold?** When you place an order we may place a temporary authorization on your card to confirm funds. It is not a charge and is released automatically, usually within a few business days.
- **Can I change my payment method after ordering?** Payment methods cannot be changed after an order is placed. The order would need to be canceled and re-placed, which a VoltMart support specialist can help with.
- **How do I get an invoice or receipt?** A receipt is emailed when your order ships, and is always available under "My Orders".

## Disputes
We do not process refunds, price adjustments, or charge disputes automatically. A VoltMart support specialist reviews these case by case.
