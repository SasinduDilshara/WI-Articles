# VoltMart General FAQ

## Accounts
- **How do I create an account?** Select "Sign up" on voltmart.example and register with your email address.
- **How do I reset my password?** Use the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in page; a reset link is emailed to you.
- **How do I update my shipping address?** Edit your saved addresses under "Account → Addresses". Changing an address does not change orders that have already shipped.

## Orders
- **How do I track my order?** Open "My Orders" in your account and select the order to see live tracking, or ask our support assistant with your order number and account email.
- **Can I change or cancel an order?** Orders move to fulfillment quickly, so changes and cancellations are not guaranteed and cannot be made self-service. A VoltMart support specialist can check whether it is still possible.
- **What are your support hours?** Our support team is available 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM ET, seven days a week. The assistant is available 24/7 for common questions.

## Products
VoltMart sells headphones, speakers, laptops, and accessories. Detailed specifications are listed on each product page.
