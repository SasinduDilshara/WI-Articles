# VoltMart Warranty Terms

## Coverage period
- **Headphones, speakers, and accessories:** 1-year limited warranty from the date of delivery.
- **Laptops (VoltBook series):** 2-year limited warranty from the date of delivery.

## What is covered
The limited warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use, including:
- Hardware failures that are not caused by the customer.
- Battery defects that drop capacity below 70% within the coverage period.

## What is NOT covered
- Accidental damage (drops, spills, cracked screens).
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications.
- Normal cosmetic wear (scratches, scuffs).
- Lost or stolen devices.
- Consumable parts such as ear tips.

## Making a warranty claim
Warranty claims require proof of purchase and are reviewed by a VoltMart support specialist, who will arrange a repair or replacement if the claim is approved. Accidental damage may be eligible for paid repair instead.
